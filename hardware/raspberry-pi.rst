Raspberry Pi
============  

The `Raspberry Pi <http://www.raspberrypi.org/>`_ is a credit-card sized computer that plugs into your TV and a keyboard. It is a capable little computer which can be used in electronics projects, and for many of the things that your desktop PC does, like spreadsheets, word-processing and games. It also plays high-definition video.
